# Code Of Conduct

AuctorForge should be a useful and safe place for writers, editors, prompt designers, and developers.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas, code, and workflows rather than people.
- Respect unpublished manuscripts and private project materials.
- Keep feedback actionable.
- Be honest about AI limitations and data-flow risks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Posting another person's private writing, project files, or personal information without permission
- Encouraging plagiarism, platform abuse, or evasion of contractual obligations
- Spamming issues or pull requests with unrelated promotions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content; close issues; or limit participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
